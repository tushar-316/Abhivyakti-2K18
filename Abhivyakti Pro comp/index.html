Choreography Cell

Dance is a performing art form of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. The movement has aesthetic and symbolic value and is acknowledged as dance by the performers and observers of a particular culture. We as the choreography cell of SRMCEM categorize dance and describe it by its choreography by its repertoire of movements.

News Paper Cell

It is a cell that is dedicated to cover all of the on-going activities during Abhivyakti and publishes a newspaper later. It’s work starts from the day when all the performing cell starts their preparations n practices to perform in Abhivyakti. We publish 3 newspapers namely Pre- Abhivyakti, Abhivyakti and Post-Abhivyakti. Pre-Abhivyakti: - In this newspaper we publish about the preparation and practices of various cells. Abhivyakti: - In this, we publish about events that are going on during the main fest. Post Abhivyakti :- In this, we publish something like feedback of all the events that had took place during the abhivyakti including star night as well. Our cells also have some of its own events. And they are very entertaining too. And this year we have added some new events as well for both students and faculties.

IPC

"IPC" is the Internal Publicity Cell of Shri Ramswaroop Memorial Group of Professional Colleges which is working for the publicity of events of Abhiyvakti 2k18, it is motivating the students to participate and enkindles in the hearts the passion to perform in various spheres. The IPC cell is one of the most creative and interactive cell of SRMGPC. This cell was brought to work in the year 2007. Since then it's been developing with great pace. It encourages the students for their keen participation and helps them in bringing out their best. This cell is well known for creating an enthusiastic ambiance in the entire college.

Infrastructure

The main work of the infrastructure cell is fulfilling all the requirements of each cell during the events. For this the members of infrastructure collects list of requirements which have to use on the stage before or during the events from the participants and performers of different cells. Performer can ask for lights, gang boxes, plane of stage, instruments, props etc. Not only stage requirements but also of the whole campus requirements of infrastructure like canopies, desks and chairs, electricity requirements etc. during fest get fulfil by infrastructure cell.

Decoration Cell

The Decoration Cell is a cell which makes the base and puts life to the fests of SRMGPC. It is one of the best off stage cell and has won many awards. This cell in our college originated in the year 2005. Since then it is becoming more and more creative. Every member of the cell has a unique and tremendous talent which motivates new talents to come and be the part of this family. Altogether we make the college lighten up and beautiful.
